Watch Us Make Something

CW: mental wellness, depression, anxiety, death/loss

Maker. Builder. Crafter. Before the pandemic, these words would not have defined us. Sure, we have both “made” things throughout our lives, but we never considered ourselves makers. It is now part of our identity. Making brings us joy. It also brings us frustration at times. Overwhelmingly, it now “makes” us who we are. Making is an act of service and love to each other and to our house. It is now tied to our core selves and to our wellbeing, and for that we are grateful.
